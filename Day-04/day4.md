# Day 4 - Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## Overview

Today, I learned about Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting, a technique that encourages AI models to reason through problems step-by-step before generating answers. This approach improves accuracy, logical thinking, and personalization.
